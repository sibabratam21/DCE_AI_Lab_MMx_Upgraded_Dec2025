Week: Week starting date (Monday) for each observation
DMA_Name: Name of the Designated Market Area (e.g., "New York NY")
DMA_ID: Unique identifier for the Designated Market Area
TRx: Total prescriptions filled (target variable)
TV_Spend: Spend on TV advertising campaigns ($)
TV_Impressions: Impressions delivered by TV advertising
Display_Spend: Spend on display advertising campaigns ($)
Display_Impressions: Impressions delivered by display advertising
PaidSearch_Spend: Spend on paid search campaigns ($)
PaidSearch_Clicks: Clicks generated by paid search campaigns
HCPCalls_Spend: Spend on HCP sales calls/rep activities ($)
HCPCalls_Count: Number of sales rep calls made to HCPs
Speaker_Spend: Spend on speaker events and programs ($)
Speaker_Events: Number of speaker events conducted
HCPEmail_Spend: Spend on HCP email campaigns ($)
HCPEmail_Sends: Number of promotional emails sent to HCPs
HCPSocial_Spend: Spend on HCP social media campaigns ($)
HCPSocial_Engagements: Engagements from HCP-targeted social media
Copay_Redemptions: Number of copay redemptions in the week
Lagged_TRx: TRx value from the prior week (used as control)
DMA_Population: Population size of the DMA
Competitor_Spend: Estimated spend from competitors in the same category ($)
Seasonality: Seasonal adjustment factor (cyclical pattern over year)
